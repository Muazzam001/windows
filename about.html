ABOUT BROW WINDOWS

Windows, Doors & Conservatories.

WHAT WE DO

With a wealth of experience in the trade we are able to provide a quality service to all of our customers, each step of the way we are there keeping you up to date with the latest styles to compliment your home.

We offer a range of double glazing, energy efficient units and styles which is perfect for modernising any home. Here at Brow Windows we also provide other services such as composite doors, these come in a variety of styles from traditional to contemporary, as well as spacious conservatories which is great for extra living space or transforming into whatever suits your needs.
Brow Windows have been installing top quality Double Glazing & Conservatories from their base in Altrincham Cheshire. Here at Brow Windows, you’ll find although we are competitive on price, we do not compromise on quality with any of our Double Glazing installations.
